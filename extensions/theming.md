# Theming

Theming extensions focus on the aesthetics of the SillyTavern UI. They may be highly dependent on specific SillyTavern versions and could be quite prone to breaking on upgrade.

## Extensions

- [CSS Snippets](https://github.com/LenAnderson/SillyTavern-CssSnippets)
  - Provides an in-interface means for customising bits of CSS to adjust look and feel
